About Me

My name is Muhammad Nadeem. I am working as a Research Scientist-Computer Vision at CVPR, Seoul, South Korea, working on Cutting-edge AI Research on image classification, object detection and segmentation tasks. I received my MS degree from Sejong University, advised by Prof. Hyeonjoon Moon. As a Master student I worked as a student researcher at CVPR Lab, working on deep learning research for sewer defect classification, image compression, face recognition and vision transformer.

PhD Plan (In Future): I am aiming to work on learning with noisy labels, data augmentation methods, contrastive learning, etc. In addition, I have also conducted extensive researches on face recognition, person search, person re-identification and vision transformer.
